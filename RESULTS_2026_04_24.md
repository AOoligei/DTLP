## Key method findings

1. **Trick B (`--pair_recency_feat`) + `hard_neg_ratio` threshold law**:
   - Low pair-recurrence datasets (ρ < 10%): trick B + hard-neg 0.20-0.25 gives **massive lift** (comment +1.5%, coin +1.4%)
   - High pair-recurrence (ρ > 40%): hard-neg **hurts** by 10-20% (wiki -13%, yago -77%)
   - Threshold ~10% ρ confirmed across all datasets

2. **CRAFT+PAS beats DTLP on THG datasets** (thgl-software demo): DTLP is diagnostic probe, backbone+PAS gives bigger headroom when DTLP plateaus.

3. **Node-pred (tgbn-*) ceiling is tight**: 0.7953 on trade, 0.389 on token — both bounded by data sparsity not capacity.
